Classification Scheme for Written Works
The Classification Scheme for Written works is a system of serial numbers on information sheets for printed and digital works published by OB. It is a system similar to the Dewey Decimal Classification, utilizing the following configuration:

A - 00 - 000

Wherein:
Section
Meaning
A
Series of the work
00
Category of the work
000
Number of the work


The following categories exist:

00 - Manual Pages
01 - Historiographies
02 - Essays
03 - Marx and Engels’ Works
04 - Lenin’s Works
05 - Onwards, Barbarians! Periodical Issues
