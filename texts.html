Note - 15th of July 2022
  • Audio Archival Project

We have recently received a box of audio CDs, and we have been asked to archive the contents of the salvageable ones in exchange for keeping the cases. Thus begins our audio archival project. Results will be posted to archive.org, and we will archive them as we go. CDs seem to mostly consist of brazilian sertanejo music.
